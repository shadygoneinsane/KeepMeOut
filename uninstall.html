The easiest way to uninstall the app is to Switch off the Device Administrator

Find the switch for device administrator like one encircled in Red in the screenshot below and switch if off and then you can Uninstall the app


Device Administrator switch is encircled in red
If that Switch is not accessible there is another way to Remove the Device Administrator settings :
Step 1: Go to Settings
Step 2: Click Security (Under personal set of settings)
Step 3: Click Device Administrators (Under Device administration)
Step 4: Then remove Device Administration settings from Keep Me Out ( uncheck the checkbox)
